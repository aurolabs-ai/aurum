#### 1. Regulatory Compliance as a Core Strategic Function

**Implication:**  
Compliance is no longer a peripheral concern but a core strategic function for European AI startups. Startups must allocate significant resources to legal, technical, and operational compliance, potentially diverting capital and talent from product development and market expansion [Bruegel](https://www.bruegel.org/policy-brief/catch-us-or-prosper-below-tech-frontier-eu-artificial-intelligence-strategy?utm_source=openai). This is especially acute for high-risk AI systems, where the cost and complexity of compliance are highest.
